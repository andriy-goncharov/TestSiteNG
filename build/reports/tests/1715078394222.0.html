THE KEY

Free up the time with an event automatization

Automated webinars (evergreen webinars) are pre-recorded webinars with presentations or videos. Unlike live webinars with set times and live speakers, viewers can watch these anytime on demand or at a scheduled time. However, automated webinars can still be reliable and interactive, with features like virtual and live chat, polls, Q&As, and calls to action to boost engagement. They are famous for everything from marketing to training because they're easy to scale and provide valuable insights into audience behavior.

How can I set up an automated webinar?
Easily set up automated webinars with our brand-new software for event automatization. Record a real-time webinar or upload a video, then navigate to the "Calendar" tab and click the "New automated webinar" button. Follow the prompts to customize and schedule your event.
How interactive are automated webinars?
Your webinars can be surprisingly interactive on autopilot! That is why our free automated webinar platform includes features like virtual bots, chat, and Q&A emulation to enhance audience engagement and ROI. Yes, of course. We provide comprehensive analytics when you automate your webinars, including attendee behavior, engagement, and more, to help you improve future events to generate leads on autopilot. You can also extend base in-build reporting with Google Tag Manager integration.

Yes, of course! Our software for your automated events is designed for accessibility across devices, including laptops, tablets, and smartphones.
